Number of Moves: 0

Instructions

  1. The game is won when the numbers are in counting order (from left to right and all the way down), with the empty block at the bottom right corner.
  2. The "Start" button starts the game; it resets the number of moves back to 0 and starts counting your moves all over again.
  3. The "Reset" button pauses the game, and the game would stop counting your moves UNTIL you click "Start" again.
  4. Before you "Start" the game, you can manually shuffle the puzzle, or click the "Shuffle" button to randomly shuffle.
